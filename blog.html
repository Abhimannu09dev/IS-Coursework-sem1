    About Technology

    The technology is the application of scientific knowledge, skills, tools and technology which help to fulfill human needs and improve our ability to control and understand the natural world. The goal of technology is to help and improve the efficiency and accuracy of the work.

    Positive Impact of Technology in our Daily Life

    The technology is constantly being used in our day to day life. As all things both have positive and negative impact. The positive impact of technology in our day to day life are mentioned bellow:

    • The technology helps to connect to people from all over the world.
    • The technology helps us to get jobs in all over the world like remote jobs for IT students.
    • The technology helps us to share knowledge from all over the world.
    • The technology helps us to know more about the body and give report of our health.
    • The technology helps us to gain knowledge in any circumstances. For example: Online classes during Corona period

    Negative Impact of Technology in our Daily Life

    The technology is constantly being used in our day to day life. As all things both have positive and negative impact. The negative impact of technology in our day to day life are mentioned bellow:

    • The technology makes some of the people so addict that they never go out and always operate technological devices.
    • Due to the overuse of technological devices the user might get various health problems like shortsightness farsightedness,.
    • The technology is getting smarter day by day which leads to overtake the jobs of people.
    • The technology might mess with the sleep of many people. For example: Late night Netflix and Chill programs.
    • The technology can mislead people as people can post many fictions such that people might get confused.

    Impact of technology on daily life and business/printing business

    As already told above the technology can have positive impact in life if used properly. The impacts of technology in business are:

    • The technology helps to automize daily business tasks like data entry of orders and delivery done.
    • With the help of technology the business could be conducted in online platforms like printease.org .
    • With the help of technology there is a probability of digital marketing.
    • The technology helps to preserve our data and information's.
    • The technology enables business to have online customer support platforms.

    Conclusion

    There are both good and bad of each and every thing in the world. The technology is also same i.e. it has both positive and negative impact in the world. The proper way to use technology is to focus on positive part rather than negative. It is our use that makes technology helpful in day to day life. In conclusion, the continuous evolution of technology presents both opportunities and challenges. Being in a balance between innovation and responsible use of technology will better our society.
